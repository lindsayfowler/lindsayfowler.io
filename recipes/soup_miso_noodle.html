Miso Noodle Soup

A healthy Japanese style soup that's cheap and easy to make in Australia.

Ingredients:

  • Red Miso Paste
  • Optional: ½ sachet of Dashi (bonito/kelp)
  • 1 small bunch Bok Choy
  • Carrot
  • Mushrooms
  • Cabbage
  • Noodles
  • Wakame (dried seaweed)

Method:

  1. Bring water to a simmer and add a tablespoon of miso paste.
  2. Add circles of carrot.
  3. Add chopped Bok Choy stalks
  4. Add mushrooms.
  5. Add noodles.
  6. When noodles are cooked, add chopped Bok Choy leaves and simmer for 30 seconds more.
  7. Pour into bowl and top with Wakame.
