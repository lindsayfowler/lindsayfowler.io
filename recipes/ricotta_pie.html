Ricotta Pie

Ingredients:

  • 500g Ricotta
  • 2 sheets of Pastry
  • 4 Eggs
  • ½cup of Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Nutmeg
  • 3 Tbl Spoons of Grated Parmeson
  • 1 Tbl Spoon of Parsley

Method:

  1. Beat eggs, mix with Ricotta.
  2. Mix everything in, including parsley and cheese.
  3. Grease dish with a tiny bit of butter and a bit of flour. Line with pastry.
  4. Pour in filling. Cover with pastry..
  5. Use the remaining filling to make asatart ( small Maltese pies -1 sheet makes 4 asatart)
