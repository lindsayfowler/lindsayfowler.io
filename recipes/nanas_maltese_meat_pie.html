Nana's Maltese Meat Pie

Ingredients:

    (Makes 2 pies)Ruff pastry large slices1Kg of Mince5 large Onions Optional: Add 200g bacon (remove fat) 1 flat teaspoon of CurryTomato Paste
  • 500g Ricotta
  • 2 sheets of Pastry
  • 4 eggs???
  • ½cup of Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 3 Tbl Spoons of Grated Parmeson
  • 1 Tbl Spoon of Parsley

Method:

  1. Fry onions in oil.
  2. Add mince and bacon, stirring until brown.
  3. Add two flat tablespoons of tomato paste.
  4. Add 1/2 a cup of tomato sauce and water.
  5. Add salt and pepper.
  6. Add 1 flat teaspoon of curry.
  7. Add peas and cook slowly
  8. Line dish with pastry and sprinkle with a little flour.
  9. Add filling and add pastry to the top. Edge with a fork. Put holes in top.
  10. Cook on the middle shelf at 230C for 15 min and then at 190C for 25 min....
