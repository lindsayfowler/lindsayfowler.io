Almond Ricciarelli Cookies

Delicious sweet almond lemon bricks, perfect with coffee. Very easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 300g Almond Meal
  • Zest of one Lemon
  • Icing Sugar for dusting
  • 250g of Caster Sugar
  • 2 large egg whites

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. In a large bowl, mix almond meal and sugar.
  3. Add egg whites and mix with hands.
  4. Pinch 35g quantities into brick shapes and roll in icing sugar until they are covered and no longer sticky.
  5. Bake in oven at 180°C on a tray for 10-12 min or until cookies crack and brown.
  6. Cool on a rack, they keep for weeks -as if they would ever gain the opportunity to prove this...
    7. It may be wise to create portuguese custard tarts with the left over yokes.
