Chocolate Blueberry Brownies

I guess I'll claim this one as mine. Originally Chocolate Brownies from mum's recipe tin, the easiet $2 sugar fix when I was a teen. I removed the icing, reduced the butter and added blueberries - so this is a good one from Winter to Spring. The budget is upped to 5 bucks and Jeezus, is it an improvement.. stare in wonder at the blueberries as they come out of the oven a wicked purple popping from a marbled surface of simple chocolate cake.

Ingredients

  • 90g Butter
  • 1/4 cup of Cocoa
  • 3/4 cup of Castor Sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup Plain Flour
  • Punnet of Blueberries. (alternatively 3/4 cup chopped Pecans)

Method:

  1. Melt butter over low heat in metal saucepan, which you can mix all other ingredients in.
  2. Stir in sifted cocoa and sugar.
  3. Remove from heat, add eggs, mix well.
  4. Stir in sifted flour.
  5. Cut a circle of baking paper and pop it in an 18cm circular spring form tin.
    6. >
  6. Alternate between pouring some of the thick mixture into the tin and thowing ins some blueberries reserving a generous amount drop on the top.
  7. Spread evenly over base of tin.
  8. Bake in moderate oven for around 25 min; still a bit gooey inside if you can.
  9. Stand 10 min.
  10. Cut in tin when warm or cold
