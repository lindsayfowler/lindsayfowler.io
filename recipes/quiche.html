Quiche

Ingredients:

  • 5 Eggs
  • A large Onion
  • Pastry - frozen puff pastry is fine - typically 1½ sheets
  • 200mL of Lite Cream
  • 80-100g block of Cheese - any cheddar or similar - don't use cancerous pre-grated cheese in a plastic bag..
  • Olive Oil
  • A Zucchini for filling or ⅛ or Leek
    • Optional toppings or extras:
  • Slices of tomato with Olives
  • Egyptian Dukkah
  • Asparagus and Tomato
  • Salmon (inside) and Dill on top
  • Serve with a Salad or roasted Vegtables or Mashed Potato and Asparagus, etc.

Method:

  1. Defrost the pastry, preheat oven to 180C and cook potatoes first if desired.
  2. Dice the onion and Zucchini and fry in Olive Oil until translucent.
  3. Beat eggs together with a fork
  4. Grate cheese
  5. In the beaten eggs, add grated cheese, cream, and onions.
  6. Line a pie dish with a little buttery and then the pastry.
  7. Pour the quiche mixture into the lined dish
  8. Decorate with optional ingredients (listed).
  9. Top with nutmeg, ground sea salt and peppercorns
  10. Cook at 190C for 40 minutes.
  11. Serve with a Salad or roasted Vegtables.
