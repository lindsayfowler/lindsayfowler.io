Lindsay's Banana Cake

This excellent banana cake is light on sugar, it's VEGAN - and it's delicious. No, really - I'm not a vegan, so my opinion counts!.

Ingredients:

  • 3 blackening bananas
  • ½ cup Raw Sugar (or brown)
  • ⅓ cup Walnuts (or pecans)
  • ⅓ cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 cup Plain Flour
  • 1 cup Self Raising Flour

Method:

  1. Chop nuts and set aside
  2. Mash bananas.
  3. Sift in both flours
  4. Add Olive Oil
  5. Add sugar
  6. Mix Well
  7. Pour into an oiled and lined loaf cake tin
  8. Mix 2 tablespoons of raw sugar in with a good hit of cinnamon to colour the sugar
  9. Sprinkle this over the mixture in the tin and sprinkle with ¼ cup of water
  10. Bake at 190C for 26 min on the middle shelf
