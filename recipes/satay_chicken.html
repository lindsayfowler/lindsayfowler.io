Satay Chicken

Ingredients:

    Satay Chicken
  • 1 chicken
  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 1 crushed garlic clove
  • 1 dessertspoon coriander powder.
  • 2 dessertspoons mushroom soy sauce
  • 4 dessertspoons peanut butter
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • juice of one lemon
  • 1-2 dessertspoons chili sauce
  • Bananas
  • Desicated Coconut
  • Honey
  • Cucumber
  • Fruit Chutney
  • Plain Yoghurt

Method:

Chicken:
  1. Rinse the chicken in cold water.
  2. Mix all the remaining ingredients and coat the chicken with the mixture.
  3. Cover in foil. Leave to marinade for 2-3 hours or overnight.
  4. Bake at 180 C. A whole chicken takes 1 3/4 hours; chicken pieces take about 1 1/4hours..
Cook rice in the last 15min and make Yoghurt Serve With: Yoghurt, Desiccated, Coconut, Honey, Bananas, Cucumber, Fruit Chutney Yoghurt:
  1. Mix plain yoghurt and honey.
  2. Slice a banana, drop on top.
  3. Sprinkle with coconut.
