Lentil Salad

Ingredients:

  • Green Lentils
  • ¼ cup of slivered almonds
  • Pomegranate
  • sultanas
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 carrot
  • Watermelon
  • Natural Greek Style Yogurt

Method:

  1. Put lentils in pot with water covering them. Heat lentils in until cooked (they will get a little soft).
  2. Meanwhile, grate carrot, cut watermelon into cubes and set each aside in a bowl.
  3. When lentils are soft, drain them in a colander and fry them in a pan with olive oil. Add salt to taste.
  4. Add almonds, Pomegranate sultanas to the lentils.
  5. Serve all condiments on plate or keep them in seperate bowls.
