Csilla’s Pumpkin Soup

A healthy Japanese style soup that's cheap and easy to make in Australia.

Ingredients:

  • 500g of Pumpkin
  • 2½ cm of Ginger
  • 1 Shallot peeled and diced.
  • Coconut Cream
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Half a lime

Method:

  1. In a large pot, fry shallot in olive oil.
  2. Add pumpkin and ginger and fry for 10 min on medium heat.
  3. Add a litre of water and put a lid on top. Simmer for 40 min.
  4. When the pumpkin is tender, add 150g of coconut cream.
  5. Take it off the heat and blend it until smooth.
  6. Add some chilli flakes. Add the juice of half a lime.
  7. Season with Salt and Pepper. Serve with coconut cream on top and fresh mint or chives.
