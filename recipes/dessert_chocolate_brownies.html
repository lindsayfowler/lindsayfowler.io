Chocolate Brownies

A Donna Hay recipe that I optimised for less cleanup, and added raspberries.

Ingredients

  • 125g Butter
  • 125g Dark Chocolate
  • 1 cup of Castor Sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup Plain Flour
  • 2 tablespoons Self-Raising Flour
  • Punnet of Raspberries or forest fruits. (alternatively 3/4 cup chopped Pecans)

Method:

  1. Preaheat oven to 180°C
  2. Melt butter and chocolate over low heat in metal saucepan.
  3. Mix eggs and sugar.
  4. Remove from heat, add eggs, mix well.
