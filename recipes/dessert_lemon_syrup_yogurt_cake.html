Lemon Syrup Yoghurt Cake

Delicious lemon syrup cake with yogurt. Perfect with a coffee.

Ingredients:

    Cake:
  • 125g Butter
  • 1 cup Caster Sugar
  • 3 Eggs
  • Zest of ½ a Lemon
  • 200g (1½ cups) Self-Raising Flour
  • 200g Natural Yoghurt
    • Syrup:
  • ⅓ cup Water
  • 150g (¾ cup) Sugar
  • Juice of ½ a Lemon

Method:

    Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 180°.
  2. Cream the butter and caster sugar.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time.
  4. Gently fold in the lemon zest, then flour, then yohgurt.
  5. Scrape into 20cm cake tin, making a depression in the center to aid cooking the center.
  6. Set timer for 30min. Check at 30 and be prepared to leave in another 10 or 15 min.
    7. Syrup:
  7. Heat water, sugar and lemon juice in a saucepan.
  8. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  9. Carefully pour on boiling water.
  10. In the tin, poke the cooked cake with a skewer and pour syrup over.
