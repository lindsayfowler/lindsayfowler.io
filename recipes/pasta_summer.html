Summer Pasta

Ingredients:

  • Pasta spirals or rigatoni
  • 500 gms tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon capers
  • ½ cup stuffed green olives
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 2 crushed cloves of garlic
  • 1 red onion finely chopped

Method:

  1. Chop all ingredients, and leave to marinade for several hours if possible.
  2. Drain pasta and return to pot. Mix in the sauce.
  3. Cook and drain pasta and return to hot pot.
  4. Mix the sauce in.
