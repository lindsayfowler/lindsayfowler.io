Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 200g Dark Chocolate
  • 2 tablespoons Whiskey
  • 5 Eggs
  • 300mL Cream
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla

Method:

  1. Break the chocolate into small bowl and put it in the oven to melt.
  2. Allow the chocolate cool a bit and separete the egg yolks and whites.
  3. Mix the egg yolks in with chocolate (not too hot; test with finger) with a fork.
  4. Add vanilla and a short shot of espresso.
    5. .
  5. In medium bowl, beat egg whites until stiff.
  6. In large bowl beat cream until thick.
  7. Add whskey to cream.
  8. Mix chocolate into cream.
  9. Add a little egg white to chocolate mixture to lighten it's consistency.
  10. Last, mix everything into egg whites. Don't use wooden spoon.
  11. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
