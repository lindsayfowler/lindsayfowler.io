Porridge with Banana and Berries

Porridge is the best breakfast, provided you have decent ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Walnuts
  • Banana
  • Raspberries / Strawberries / Blueberries
  • Honey - Real Honey NOT Capilano fake honey!
  • Cinamon

Method:

  1. Cook oats with milk in a saucepan on medium heat, stirring often.
  2. Chop nuts and any fruit.
  3. When it bubbles, pour porridge in bowls, top with generous amounts of fruit and berries, criss-cross with honey, sprinkle with cinamon.
