Lee Bailey's Chocolate Chunk Cookies

Lee Bailey's Chocolate Chunk Cookies - virtually, shamefully, the only thing I made from this book.

Ingredients:

  • 155g Unsalted Butter
  • ½ cup of Soft Brown Sugar
  • ½ cup of Caster Sugar
  • ¼ cup of Demarrara Sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 1 teaspoon Bicarb of Soda
  • ½ teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 ½ caps of vanilla essence
  • ¾ cup of chopped Peacans
  • 150-250g of chopped Dark Chocolate

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C.
  2. In a bowl, chop the butter, add sugar and place in oven to soften the butter.
  3. Take it out after a minute and cream the butter and sugar.
  4. Crack the egg in a seperate bowl (a bad egg could spoil your plan).
  5. Add the 2 cups of Flour, and the teaspoon of Bi-carb Soda, and the ½ teaspoon of Salt, and the 1 ½ caps of vanilla essence and the egg.
  6. Chop up the Peacans if you are using them.
  7. Chop up the chocolate or use choc chips and put it in.
  8. Spoon 3 x 4 rows on an ungreased tray.
  9. Cook on the top shelf for 11 minutes precisely.
