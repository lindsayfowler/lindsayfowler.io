Rittienne Caruana's Ravjul tal-Irkotta

Ingredients:

    Ingredients: for Dough
  • Plain white flour (UK), 850 g
  • Simolina by Aquiljoe, 150 gram(s)
  • Egg, fresh, 6 medium
  • Salt, 1 dash
    • Ingredients: for Filling
  • Ricotta Cheese, part skim milk, 500 grams
  • Simolina by Aquiljoe, 100 gram(s)
  • Salt and Pepper , 1 dash
  • Parsley, 1 tbsp
    • Ingredients: for Sause
  • Bella Travola Pure Tomato Paste, 120 grams
  • Garlic powder, 1 tsp
  • Parmesan Cheese, grated, 25 grams
  • 1 No Raviola approx 17grams - 19grams
  • Half a small Onionfor each person
  • 1 pear for each person
  • 2 teaspoons Raw Sugar for each person
  • Parmesan cheese sliced into thin squares
  • Olive Oil
  • Ground Rock saltl and Pepper corns

Method:

  1. Mix flour and semolina, make a well in the centre, add beaten eggs, olive oil and salt and using your fingers, start incorporating the flour into the flour. Knead until you have a soft pliable dough, cover in plastic wrap and refrigerate.
    2. Filling:.
  2. Mix all the ingredients together.
  3. Cut the dough in 4 pieces and roll out each piece into a wide ribbon shape. do not roll out the dough too thin because this isn't a very strong dough. You can use a pasta machine or a rolling pin.
  4. Put 1 tbsp of filling on 2 of the dough leaving them a bit spaced out. lay another sheet of pasta on top and using your thumbs press aroung the filling to make sure that the ravioli are sealed shut. Cut using a biscuit cutter cut out the ravioli and lay on more semolina. The remaining cut out pasta can be re kneaded and filled too.
  5. Do not leave lying around for a long time ofr they will stick to each other.
  6. To cook boil in salted water for 4 minutes ( but not a rolling boil).
  7. To Freeze: freeze immediatley on a baking sheet dusted with semolina, once frozen pack in bags.
  8. Serve topped with a plain tomato sauce and 1 tbsp of parmesan cheese sprinkled on top. Drizzle a bit of olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
