Drambuie Cake

This rich chocolate cake with a gooey center will not disappoint. Perfect with coffee or as a dessert.

Ingredients:

  • ⅓ cup Prunes
  • ¾ cup Peacans
  • ¼ cup Drambuie
  • 200g Dark Chocolate
  • ⅔ cup Castor Sugar
  • 125g Unsalted Butter
  • 3 Eggs (seperated)
  • ¾ cup Plain Flour

Method:

  1. Chop ⅓ cup prunes & soak in ¼ cup Drambuie.
  2. Melt 200g dark chocolate and leave to cool.
  3. Mix into chocolate 125g of unsalted butter
  4. In a seperate bowl beat ⅔ cup Castor Sugar into 3 egg yolks
  5. Mix chocolate mixture into egg yolk mixture
  6. Add ¾ cup Plain Flour
  7. Add prune mixture
  8. Add pecans
  9. In a large bowl, beat egg whites with a pinch of salt until firm.
  10. Fold egg whites into cake mixture
  11. Pour into a butterred and lined 18cm cake tin
  12. Bake at 190C for 26 min on the middle shelf
