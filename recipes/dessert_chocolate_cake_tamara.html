Tamara's Chocolate Cake

The famous sour cream chocolate cake.

Ingredients:

  • 200g Butter
  • 1 cup water
  • ¾cup cocoa
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Bicarb of Soda
  • 1 teaspoon of Baking Powder
  • 1 ½ caps of vanilla essence
  • ¾ cup sour cream

Method:

  1. Melt 200g Margarine, 1 cup water, 3/4 cup cocoa in a saucepan and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, thoroughly mix the following, avoid lumps: 2 eggs, 2 cups plain flour, 1 teaspoon Bicarb of Soda, 1 teaspoon of Baking Powder, 1 1/2 caps of vanilla essence, 3/4 cup sour cream and 2 cups of sugar.
  4. SLOWLY add the contents of the saucepan to the bowl (slowly to avoid lumps).
  5. Grease and line a tin and cook at 350°F or 176.7°C. 2 loaf tins (55 min) or 1 large tin (1 3/4 hours? Note:probably too long and will dry out) or muffin tins (45 min) Decrease temperature towards the end. Use a cake tester. Allow tin to cool before tipping
