My Crispy Skin Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 1 Piece of Salmon per person
  • Olive Oil
    • Option 1 Mash and Veg:
  • Asparagus
  • Potato (1 small per person)
  • Sweet Potato (1 piece per person)

Method:

  1. Put on pot of water to boil for potato
  2. Peel potatos and cook in water.
  3. Heat a generous amount of oil in a pan.
  4. Wash the salmon in water and dry with a clean towel.
  5. Cook salmon with a sizzle, skin side first, in hot oil, ensuring it does not stick
  6. When cooked halfway through, flip each salmon piece once and once only.
  7. Fry Asparagus spears with the salmon.
  8. Drain and mash potatoes with butter and milk, taking care to seperate the two kinds.
  9. Serve mash potato in orange and white, topped with salmon and then deep green asparagus spears. Drizzle a bit of olive oil from the pan, season with salt and pepper.
