Self-saucing Chocolate Pudding

An economical dessert that will still impress most warm-hearted people. Sourced from the Australian Women's Weakly Cookbook, this was always a killer growing up in my family.

Ingredients:

    Pudding
  • 1 cup SR flour
  • 2 teaspoons cocoa
  • ½cup (95 g) butter
  • ½cup caster sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
    • Sauce:
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa
  • 1 1/4 cups boiling water

Method:

    Pudding:
  1. Mix the butter and sugar in a casserole dish.
  2. Add the beaten egg.
  3. Sift in the flour and cocoa.
  4. Add the milk.
    5. Now for the sauce:
  5. Mix the sugar and cocoa
  6. sprinkle on top of pudding.
  7. Carefully pour on boiling water.
  8. Bake at 190 C , second shelf for 45 mins.
