Lemon Delicious Pudding

  • 2 Lemons
  • 60g butter
  • 1 ½ cups Caster Sugar
  • 4 eggs Separated
  • 4 tablespoons Self-Raising Flour
  • 1 ½cups Milk
  • A pinch of Salt
  • Icing Sugar for dusting cream to serve

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180 C.
  2. Butter a large casserole dish.
  3. Grate the rind of 1 lemon.
  4. Juice the 2 lemons.
  5. Cream the butter, lemon zest and the sugar.
  6. Add the egg yolks, flour and milk.
  7. Add the lemon juice.
  8. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg whites with a pinch of salt until stiff and fold gently into the batter.
  9. Pour mixture into casserole. Place casserole in a large baking dish.
  10. Pour hot water into baking dish until it it is half way up the sides of the casserole.
  11. Place in oven and bake for 1 hour. Take care when removing from the oven.
